# Validation Plan

## Week 1: Concierge MVP

### Day 1
- Prepare landing page/demo
- Prepare sample CSV template
- Prepare manual report template

### Day 2-3
- Contact 10 restaurant/cafe owners
- Offer free 7-day AI daily report
- Ask for exported POS CSV or screenshots

### Day 4-7
- Send daily Telegram/WhatsApp summary manually
- Track owner replies and questions
- Collect objections and testimonial language

## Discovery questions

1. What POS do you use?
2. Can you export daily sales CSV?
3. What do you check every night now?
4. What takes the most time: menu, staff, reviews, inventory, social posts, cost control?
5. If I save you 2 hours/week or help sell more, what would you pay monthly?

## Red flags

- Owner cannot export data and does not want to share screenshots
- Wants full POS replacement immediately
- Expects accounting/payroll/legal compliance in v1

## Best first niche

Sydney Asian restaurants and cafes where multilingual menu/review/social content is painful.
